Featured Work

VELA

VELA is a wind, wave, and weather app designed for water-sport enthusiasts. My iteration of the VELA app sets itself apart form the competition by offering a live weather map with radar overlays and a home screen widget.

Ruminate

Ruminate is a vocabulary acquisition and retention app designed for users who want to engage in continuous learning. Ruminate sets itself apart from the competition by allowing users to engage with each other using games and shared lists.

Mischief

Mischief is an online board game. Mischief blends elements of card games, board games, and table-top role playing games. Mischief's theme and setting are inspired by classic table top games like Dungeons & Dragons and modern role playing games like The Elder Scrolls series.
